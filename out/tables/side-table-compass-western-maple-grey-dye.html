Western maple (grey)

A thick round top covered in a quarter-matched pattern of one of the world's most dramatic woods, quilted western maple. Charcoal grey dyes accentuate the heavy figure of the quilted maple. Legs poke right through the top, providing a visual and tactile counterpoint. Base made of ebonized local hardwood.

Height 24"   Diameter 19"

Quilted Western maple top (dyed charcoal grey), ebonized Western maple legs

Finished with catalyzed lacquer
