Birdseye maple

This was developed to accompany the coffee table and side table designs that have their legs breaking through the top. One of the trickier things about it is that the angle of the table edge gradually changes from that at the front and back to that at either end, in order to match the different angles at which the legs meet the top at these places.

Length 50.25"   Depth 15.75"   Height 32"

Birdseye maple top dyed charcoal, ebonized Western maple legs

Finished with catalyzed lacquer
