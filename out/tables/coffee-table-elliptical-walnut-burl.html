Claro walnut burl

Legs poking through the top provide a visual and tactile counterpoint to the rich patterns of some of the world's most beautiful woods. The design was adapted from the Sunburst elliptical coffee table, where the base is smaller and the legs do not come through the top.

Length 50.75"   Width 28.25"   Height 15"

Claro walnut burl top, black walnut trim, ebonized ash legs

Finished with conversion varnish
