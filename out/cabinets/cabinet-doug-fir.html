Doug the cabinet

A low-slung arch supports a long, narrow, and steel-reinforced cantilevered cabinet. Very straight-grained Douglas fir in rich orangey hues. Features handmade solid brass handles. Hand-cut dovetails on the three drawers.

Length 63"   Depth 20"   Height 24.25"

Quartered Douglas fir, red alder drawer boxes, brass

Finished with conversion varnish
