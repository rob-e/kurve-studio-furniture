Swiss cream cabinet

The crisp juxtaposition of richly figured Claro walnut against a white background. Features solid wood drawer boxes and drawer dividers, and full-extension slides. Handles are handmade, black walnut on stainless steel posts. Back on centre portion can be removed for easy access to media wiring. Its companion piece is the Swiss cream coffee table.

Length 62.75"   Depth 20.75"   Height 22.75"

Claro walnut, painted FSC-certified maple plywood, stainless steel, BC red alder

Finished with a low-VOC and a very durable catalyzed paint
