Bowed-front liquor cabinet

Thick, coopered slabs support a bowed-front cabinet. Charcoal dyes accentuate the heavy birdseye figure of the maple. Features handmade brass handles and hand-cut half-blind dovetails on the interior drawer's curved front. One of two cabinets included in Lark Books' 500 Cabinets in 2010.

Width 25.5"   Depth 17.5"   Height 42.75"

Birdseye maple, poplar, western (bigleaf) maple, ebony

Finished with aniline dyes and catalyzed lacquer

Photos by Goran Basaric.
