Arc credenza

One of the very first arched pieces I made. This cabinet has both a bowed front and a bowed back, which doubles the difficulty of making it. Features hand-cut half-blind dovetails on the 3 bowed-front interior drawers.

Width 60.5"   Depth 24"   Height 42.25"

Curly african cherry, ebonized poplar, european beech, jotoba

Catalyzed lacquer finish
